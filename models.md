# rembg Models Reference

Reference list of rembg-supported AI segmentation models, including their purpose, strengths, weaknesses, and performance characteristics.

---

## Quick Comparison

| Model | Speed | Quality | Best For |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| u2netp | Very Fast | Medium | Lightweight usage |
| silueta | Very Fast | Low | Low-memory environments |
| u2net | Medium | High | General purpose |
| u2net_human_seg | Medium | High | Human portraits |
| isnet-general-use | Medium | High | Modern general segmentation |
| isnet-anime | Medium | High | Anime / cartoon images |
| birefnet-general-lite | Fast | High | Balanced performance |
| birefnet-general | Medium-Slow | Very High | High-quality general removal |
| birefnet-portrait | Medium-Slow | Very High | Portrait-focused segmentation |
| birefnet-massive | Slow | Extremely High | Maximum accuracy |
| birefnet-hrsod | Slow | Extremely High | High-resolution object detection |
| birefnet-cod | Slow | Specialized | Concealed object detection |
| birefnet-dis | Medium-Slow | Very High | Fine object segmentation |
| sam | Very Slow | Extremely High | Advanced segmentation |
